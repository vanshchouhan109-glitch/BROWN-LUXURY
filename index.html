Luxury Services Menu

Discover our range of premium pedicure and foot care services, designed to pamper, heal, and beautify.

Luxury Spa Pedicure

Our signature treatment. Includes a soothing foot bath, meticulous nail and cuticle care, callus removal, and a relaxing massage.

Medical Pedicure

Expert care for specific foot problems. We treat ingrown nails, corns, severe calluses, and other conditions safely and hygienically.

Gel Polish

Beautiful, long-lasting color. We offer a wide range of premium gel polishes that keep your toes looking perfect for weeks.

Foot Massage & Reflexology

Melt away stress with our deep relaxation foot massage, improving circulation and promoting overall wellness.

Exfoliation & Mask

Renew your skin with our gentle sugar scrubs and deeply hydrating foot masks for baby-soft results.
